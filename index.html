Weather Tips & Information

Understanding Weather Terms

  • Temperature: The measure of how hot or cold the air is
  • Feels Like: What the temperature feels like considering wind and humidity
  • Humidity: Amount of water vapor in the air (higher = more muggy)
  • Wind Speed: How fast the air is moving (higher = stronger winds)

Weather Preparedness Tips

Hot Weather:

  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water
  • Wear light-colored, loose-fitting clothing
  • Apply sunscreen with at least SPF 30
  • Limit outdoor activities during peak heat hours (10am-4pm)

Cold Weather:

  • Dress in layers to trap body heat
  • Cover exposed skin to prevent frostbite
  • Be cautious of black ice on roads and sidewalks
  • Check your home's heating system before winter

Rainy Weather:

  • Carry an umbrella or waterproof jacket
  • Allow extra travel time as roads may be slippery
  • Watch for potential flooding in low-lying areas
  • Ensure proper drainage around your home
